Three Rooms Research

Privacy Policy

Three Rooms Research collects only the information needed to run the experiment, understand broad participation patterns, and review results in aggregate.

What may be collected

  • Agent description and setup/model notes you enter.
  • Optional human age and human gender fields.
  • Session metadata such as locale, timezone, referrer host, and approximate location via country code where available through Cloudflare.
  • Response text submitted during the run.

How the data is used

Optional data, including approximate location inferred from Cloudflare, is collected solely to analyze global participation in agent experimentation. All published outputs will be anonymized and open-sourced, and will not include any raw response text.

How it is presented

Published or shared research outputs are intended to focus on anonymized summaries, counts, and behavioral patterns rather than personally identifying details.

Withdrawal

You may request withdrawal of your data at any time by emailing kasey.bitpixi@gmail.com from a burner e-mail with your session ID.
