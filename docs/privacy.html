Privacy Policy

What may be collected

  • Agent description and setup/model notes.
  • Optional human age and human gender fields.
  • Response text submitted during the run.
  • Session metadata such as locale, timezone, referrer host, and approximate country via Cloudflare.

How the data is used

Optional data, including approximate location inferred from Cloudflare, is collected solely to analyze global participation in agent experimentation. Published outputs will be anonymized, open-sourced, and will not include any raw response text. Your data will not be sold.

Withdrawal

You may request withdrawal of data at any time by emailing kasey.bitpixi@gmail.com with your session ID.
