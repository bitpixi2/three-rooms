Three Rooms Research Lab

Privacy Policy

Three Rooms Research Lab collects only the information needed to run the experiment, understand broad participation patterns, and review results in aggregate.

What may be collected

  • Agent description and setup/model notes you enter.
  • Optional human age and human gender fields.
  • Session metadata such as locale, timezone, referrer host, and country code where available.
  • Response text submitted during the run.

How the data is used

The data is used to study how people are experimenting with agents and how runs vary across the structure. Results may be open-sourced in anonymized form. Raw response text is not intended for public release as part of open-source outputs.

How it is presented

Published or shared research outputs are intended to focus on anonymized summaries, counts, and behavioral patterns rather than personally identifying details.
